Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa that truly pulses with life; as the most populous nation on the continent, it’s a vibrant tapestry woven from over 250 distinct ethnic groups. Looking back, it held the title of one of Africa’s fastest-growing economies as recently as 2015, but sadly, years of poor leadership have taken a heavy toll, leaving the economy in a fragile state. Beyond its economic struggles, Despite the many challenges, there is a palpable sense of resolve in the air as the country looks toward the upcoming 2027 elections, there is a strong, collective intent among Nigerians to push for the meaningful change they want to see for their future.
